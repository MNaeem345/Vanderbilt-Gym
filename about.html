What We Offer at Vanderbilt Gym

Here at Vanderbilt Gym, we offer a multitude of equipment revolving around those interested in boxing and Mixed Martial Art(MMA). Our motto is to live healthy and live a balance life and our belief in doing that is learning basic self-defense. We offer every gold member a free trainer twice a week and no matter the skill difference of our members, we treat everyone with respect and as comrades. Join us for your journey of self development and self growth here at Vanderbilt Gym, where monsters are born.
